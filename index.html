Bruce Wayne

Billionaire philanthropist by day. Gotham's guardian by night. After witnessing his parents' murder as a child, Bruce Wayne vowed to wage war on crime.

🔍

The Detective

The World's Greatest Detective. Batman's mind is his most powerful weapon — capable of solving the unsolvable and anticipating every move his enemies make.

⚡

The Warrior

Master of over 127 martial arts disciplines. Peak human physical condition. No superpowers — just years of relentless training and unbreakable willpower.

Grapple Gun

Fires a grappling hook for rapid ascent and traversal across Gotham's skyline.

Smoke Pellets

Instant smoke screen for cover and escape. A classic in the Batman utility belt.

Batmobile

Armored, high-speed vehicle. Turbine-powered. Packed with offensive and defensive systems.

Forensic Kit

Mobile crime scene lab. DNA analysis, fingerprinting, and chemical identification.

Batsuit

Kevlar-composite armor. Fire-resistant. Bullet-resistant. Cape doubles as a glider.
